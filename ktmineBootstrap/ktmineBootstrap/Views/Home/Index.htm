ktMINE Bootstrap

ktMINE Bootstrap is a project for ktMINE applications built on Twitter Bootstrap to maintain styling consistency across products, adhere to branding, and allows developers to pay less attention to styling and more on the application itself.

Infoboxes

Infoboxes are used to contain primary content material, such as agreement summaries within LAF or RRF. Subtext portions can be duplicated as many times as necessary within an infobox. Infoboxes also come equipped with a button to hide the inner contents of the box, excluding the header and footer. Infoboxes also include accordion functionality to collapse specific sections.
